01 Who We Are

Nugget™ is operated by Anna Ludwinowski, based in Surrey, British Columbia, Canada.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at hello@annaludwinowski.com. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.

02 What Data We Collect

We collect two categories of data:

A. LinkedIn Export Data — temporary

When you upload your LinkedIn data file, it is parsed and processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This data is never uploaded to our servers. A condensed summary is sent to Anthropic's API solely to generate your reports. Once your session ends or you close the tab, this data is gone.

B. Personal Contact Information — stored

When you request your free reports, we collect your first name and email address. This is used to deliver your reports, send product updates, and notify you of new features.

03 How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes only:

  • To generate your personalized Nugget reports
  • To communicate with you about your reports and the product
  • To improve Nugget using aggregated, anonymous usage patterns only
  • To send our quarterly newsletter (you can unsubscribe any time)
We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

04 Third Parties We Work With

Running Nugget requires a small number of trusted third-party services. Here's who they are and what they touch:

Anthropic (Claude API)

Your LinkedIn data summary is processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate your reports. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models by default. Learn more at anthropic.com/privacy.

Kit (formerly ConvertKit)

Your name and email address are stored in Kit, our email platform, used solely to deliver your reports and communications from Nugget.

Vercel

Nugget is hosted on Vercel. Vercel collects standard server logs and anonymized analytics (page views, performance data). No personal data beyond standard hosting is shared with Vercel.

Make.com

We use Make.com to route your name and email from the sign-up form to Kit. Only your name and email pass through this webhook — no LinkedIn data.

05 Data Retention

  • LinkedIn export data: Not retained. Processed in-browser only and discarded at session end.
  • Name and email: Retained for as long as you are an active user or subscriber. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized. Retained by Vercel per their standard data practices.

06 Your Rights (PIPEDA)

As a Canadian business, we are governed by the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your information
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada
Note for international users: If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under GDPR. We handle all data access and deletion requests regardless of your location.

07 Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit)
  • Your LinkedIn data is never written to disk or stored on any server
  • Access to email marketing data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use reputable, security-audited third-party services

No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have concerns about a specific data handling practice, please contact us.

08 Cookies & Analytics

Nugget uses Vercel Analytics for basic, anonymized usage data — pages visited, load times, and general traffic patterns. This does not include personal identifiers. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

09 Children's Privacy

Nugget is designed for founders, business owners, and professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we'll notify you by email. Continuing to use Nugget after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
